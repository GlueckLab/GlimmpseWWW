Scale Factors for Covariance

Changes in variability can dramatically affect power. Scale factors allow you to compute power for alternative covariance values by scaling Σe. For example, entering the scale factors 0.5, 1, and 2 would compute power for Σe divided by 2, Σe matrix as entered, and Σe matrix multiplied by 2.

Type each value into the list box and click "enter" on your keyboard. To remove an item, click the "x" next to the item.

Within-Subject Contrast: $\boldsymbol{U}$ Matrix

The $\boldsymbol{U}$ matrix defines the contrasts for within-subject effects. The matrix is necessary for multivariate and repeated measures designs. The number of rows in $\boldsymbol{U}$ must equal the number of columns in $\boldsymbol{B}$. To ensure conforming matrices, the row dimension of $\boldsymbol{U}$ cannot be adjusted on this screen.

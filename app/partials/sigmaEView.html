Covariance of Errors: Σe Matrix

For each independent sampling unit, Σe is the covariance of the random errors. For univariate designs, Σe will be a 1x1 matrix containing the variance of the error term. More complex structures may be entered for multivariate or repeated measures designs. Values for Σe are typically obtained from pilot data or previous studies.

Σe is a square, symmetric matrix with dimensions equal to the number of columns in B.

Hypothesis as Contrast Matrices: Introduction

In this section, you can define the hypothesis of interest for your study. The hypothesis is defined by specifying contrasts for between-participant factors ($\boldsymbol{C}$ matrix) and for within-participant factors ($\boldsymbol{U}$ matrix).

You may also select one or more statistical tests, and specify the Type I error rate.

Lastly, you will define the matrix of values for your null hypothesis.
